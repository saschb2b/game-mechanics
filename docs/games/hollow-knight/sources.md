## Reviews and dev coverage

- IGN, Polygon, Eurogamer reviews of Hollow Knight (2017) — broadly captured the metroidvania-meets-soulslike framing.
- *Noclip* documentary on Team Cherry — multi-part dev-team interview covering the small-studio process and post-launch DLC strategy.
- *Game Maker's Toolkit* — Mark Brown's video essay on Hollow Knight's level design (one of the most-cited GMT episodes).
- *Hollow Knight* won several Best Indie / Best Metroidvania awards in 2017–18; it's the contemporary canonical 2D metroidvania alongside Ori and Axiom Verge.

## Dev interviews and material

- **Ari Gibson** (Team Cherry, art / direction) — periodic interviews on the hand-drawn animation pipeline.
- **William Pellen** (Team Cherry, programming / design) — interviews on the small-studio engineering approach.
- **David "Dave" Kazi** — composer; the Hollow Knight OST is widely cited.
- **Christopher Larkin** (additional music) — collaborator on the soundtrack.

## Caveats

- Hollow Knight 1.0 shipped February 2017; the four free DLCs (Hidden Dreams, Lifeblood, Grimm Troupe, Godmaster) added through 2018 are considered part of the canonical experience.
- Some descriptions here (charm counts, notch budget) reflect the post-DLC final state; vanilla 1.0 had slightly fewer charms.
- Silksong is in development as of writing; this entry covers Hollow Knight 1 only.
