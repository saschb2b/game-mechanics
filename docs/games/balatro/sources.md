# Sources

## Dev interviews

LocalThunk is pseudonymous and has done a small but high-quality interview circuit. The available material is enough to triangulate the design philosophy.

- [**Rogueliker — LocalThunk on an "indie take on solitaire with a poker coat of paint"**](https://rogueliker.com/balatro-interview/) — the canonical articulation of the design philosophy. The "solitaire with a poker coat of paint" framing comes from here.
- [**TouchArcade — Balatro Interview: LocalThunk on the Mobile Version, Concept, Design, the Demo's Importance, Future Plans**](https://toucharcade.com/2024/03/18/balatro-interview-mobile-port-localthunk-dlc-plans-updates-new-jokers-demo-feedback/) — long-form interview covering Big Two as the seed, the avoiding-deckbuilders stance, demo feedback, mobile port plans.
- [**Game Informer — Balatro Was Almost Called Joker Poker**](https://gameinformer.com/interview/2024/03/21/balatro-was-almost-called-joker-poker-and-other-details-from-its-creator) — late-stage naming change context, working title history, court-jester motif explanation.
- [**Rolling Stone — Balatro Creator LocalThunk Reflects On Making 2024's Best Game**](https://www.rollingstone.com/culture/rs-gaming/balatro-localthunk-interview-1235214060/) — post-launch retrospective with reflection on the unexpected commercial success.
- [**GamesRadar+ — Exploring Balatro's hype, its ingenious twists on poker, and its mysterious creator**](https://www.gamesradar.com/exploring-balatros-hype-its-ingenious-twists-on-poker-and-its-mysterious-creator/) — broader industry framing of Balatro's hype trajectory.
- [**Apple Podcasts — Balatro Creator LocalThunk Talks Inspirations and Jokers**](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/balatro-creator-localthunk-talks-inspirations-and-jokers/id1313004515?i=1000679625804) — long-form podcast interview with LocalThunk.

- [**Low Five Gaming — Balatro Review Podcast: The Poker Roguelike That Took Over Gaming**](https://www.lowfivegaming.com/blog/ep-043-balatro) — community reception arc summary.
