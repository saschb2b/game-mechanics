## Caveats on quote attribution

PoE2 design intent is mostly delivered through livestreams, podcasts, and ExileCon talks rather than written manifestos. Quotes attributed to Jonathan Rogers in this entry's mechanic pages are **paraphrased** from those sources where direct verbatim text was not findable on the pathofexile.com forums. Where exact wording matters, treat the attributions as pointing at the right *talk* even when the wording is approximate. The factual structural claims (one-death maps, weapons-grant-skills, sockets on skill gems, parallel products) are reliable.
