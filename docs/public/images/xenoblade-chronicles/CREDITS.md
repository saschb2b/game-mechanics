## Resolution notes

The three XC1 images are sourced from English-Wikipedia article fair-use uploads. **Wikipedia's non-free content policy enforces low-resolution thumbnails (max ~100,000 pixels) for screenshots**, so these are smaller than the rest. They're the best XC1 sources we have without going to community wikis (Cloudflare-walled) or capturing from own play.

The XC2/XC3/XCX images are 1280–1920px from Eurogamer's CDN at high quality, sourced from launch-era reviews and game-page galleries.
